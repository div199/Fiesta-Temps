Support Corner

FiestaTemps is a public platform to book resorts for your functions without any actual efforts.You can search for resorts on basis of date and venue and can book them in a couple of seconds...

  • Login and Sign Up

  • How do I register with FiestaTemps?
    Click on the 'Sign Up' link on the homepage of fiestatemps.com. Enter all your details and click on 'Sign Up' and you are registered with us.
  • Is it necessary to register for booking resorts?
    Although registering is not a compulsion,we would suggest you to register on our website,since it would allow you to view your entire booking history and avail the exciting offers.And it is necessary to register on our site if you want to book any resort
  • Why am I being asked for my Mobile number?
    Well,the simplest reason being that it is for the security and also so that the booking conformation can be sent to you via SMS.And,you also get the information on discounts and offers.
  • What are the benefits of registering on the site?
    By registering,you can get access to your booking history on the site.You can also opt to get information on special offers.
  • I have forgotten my password,how do I proceed?
    On the fiestatemps.com home page, enter your username in the login box on the top right hand corner, click on the Forget Password link, and the password will be emailed to you on the email address registered with us.

  • Be a Host

  • How can I be a host?
    You will be made a host by admin only.
  • How can I make a request to admin for being a host?
    You can click on 'Be a host' on fiestatemps.com homepage or footer and fill all the details and submit.
  • What admin will do?
    Admin will contact you and will make you owner and add your resort.
  • Why so, why admin will make me owner?
    This is keeping in mind the aspect that anyone can add false resorts to make money.So admin will make you admin by checking your identity.

  • Search Resort

  • How do I search a resort?
    Click on the 'search by date' and 'search by location' on the fiestatemps.com home page.
  • Can I search by resort name?
    Yes, you can search by resort name by typing the resort name in search bar on top of fiestatemps.com homepage.
  • On what basis, I can search?
    You can search on the basis of particular date and particular location.
  • What are the benefits of searching?
    By searching, you don't have to go through a list of unwanted resorts, you can directly get a list of your demanded resorts.

  • Book Resort

  • How do I book a rsort?
    You can book a resort by choosing your resort and hall and clicking on book and confirming the entries.
  • How can I hold a resort?
    You can hold a resort by clicking on hold of that particular resort but if you didn't confirmed that resort within 3 hours of hold, the resort will be shown free.
  • How my booking will get confirmed?
    The owner will confirm your request and on confirmation, you will get a message of confirmation.
  • How can I cancel my booking?
    By clicking on the cancel button on your dashboard, you can cancel your booking.

  • Add Resort

  • How can I add my resort?
    You have to contact to admin for this.
  • How will you update your resort?
    You can update the details of your resort by your own.
  • How can I add a new resort in addition to old one?
    Admin will do this stuff for you, you just have to contact admin.
  • How can I confirm my bookings of my resort?
    You will see new requests in your dashboard and you can confirm bookings from there.
