about FiestaTemps

Fiestatemps.com is a new startup, came into existence in 2017, to provide a solution to organising events.

The idea of Fiesta Temps was proposed during the summer training programme at GLA University.

It provides a solution to book resorts and hotels for different purposes without actually visiting to venues.

Now, u can book venues according to your need in just few clicks.

fiestatemps.com could be a business design around a top quality service and a great quality product.

This site not only works for business making but also for dream making. Many of the special occasions you celebrate are once during a lifetime event-therefore we ensure to provide you great services and make it a rememberence. We understand your emotional attachment for your dream occasions and here we work to make it a dream come true.

It gives you service to book a venue of your interest. We give you functionality to search for resorts according to date and location. fiestatemps.com is a complete solution to book resorts and hotels for any event.
